# Arch:Genesis — Monetization Reference
Last updated: 2026-03-16

## Currency — Frame Points (FP)

### Purchase Packs
- 100 FP — $0.99
- 300 FP — $1.99 (recommended entry pack)
- 750 FP — $3.99
- 1500 FP — $7.99
- 3000 FP — $14.99

### Frame Pass — $4.99/month
- 200 FP monthly allowance
- Unlimited battles (no cooldown)
- One free image regen per cypher per month
- 6 active cypher slots included
- Unlimited cypher storage

## Store Items and FP Costs

### Cypher Management
- Cypher slot unlock (adds 1 active slot, max 6 total) — 200 FP
- Cypher storage slot (archive beyond active limit) — 100 FP per slot
- Full kit respec (all abilities, weakness, battle profile) — 150 FP
- Partial respec (single ability change) — 75 FP
- Battle profile respec — 50 FP
- Stat reroll (fresh bonus point distribution suggestion) — 75 FP
- Single stat adjustment (per stat) — 30 FP
- Image regen (3 new variants to choose from) — 100 FP

### Battle
- Battle point refill (5 battles) — 50 FP
- Battle cooldown skip — 25 FP

## Free Tier Limits
- 3 active cypher slots
- 5 battles per cooldown period
- No passive deployment
- No cypher storage
- First stat allocation free
- First image generation free (3 variants at creation)
